Good Afternoon Shooters!

I'm happy to announce that we completed all our repairs and the club is ready to open this coming weekend. We will begin our normal operations this Saturday May 2nd.

A couple of items to note:

Memberships can be paid in the skeet shack. It's going to be a busy morning, so please give yourself a little extra time as we get settled with the new processes and Target Tag systems.

There will be a cost increase this season to the Trap & Skeet Associate Membership. The cost is now $100.00 annually. This is due to the rising cost of clay targets and delivery due to fuel price surcharges. By increasing the membership fee we are able to hold the current cost per round.

With the introduction of the Target Tag system we also have new package options for our members looking to pre‑purchase their rounds. The standard single round and 10 + 1 shooters card pricing is all still available.

2026 Member Pre‑Shot Packages

Maximize your season. Support the club. Shoot more for less.

(You must be a member with a Trap & Skeet Associate Membership to purchase packages)

The Bronze Package | $80

The Standard. Perfect for the casual weekend shooter who wants a simple, no‑fuss discount.

What you get: 11 Rounds of Trap or Skeet.

The Perk: Buy 10, get 1 FREE.

Savings: Covers your annual guest fee or a couple of boxes of shells.

The Silver Package | $150

The Monthly Regular. Designed for the shooter who outpaces the basic card but isn't at the club every single day.

What you get: 22 Rounds.

The Perk: Nearly 4 Rounds FREE.

Why it’s better: Drops your per‑round cost significantly below the standard $8 rate.

The Gold Package | $250

The Competitive Edge. For the league shooter or the family that shares a card. This is our most popular “Value” tier.

What you get: 38 Rounds.

The Perk: Includes 7 FREE Rounds.

Why it’s better: You’re essentially shooting for $6.57 per round all season long.

The Platinum Package | $500

The Heavy Hitter. You’re here every week, shooting multiple rounds, and want the best possible margin.

What you get: 80 Rounds.

The Perk: A massive 18 ROUNDS FREE.

Why it’s better: This locks in a 25% discount on your targets. Secure your supply now and don't worry about price hikes mid‑season.

The Diamond Package | $1,000

The Ultimate Supporter. This tier is for our most dedicated members who want to ensure the club’s success while securing the lowest possible rate.

What you get: 165 Rounds.

The Perk: 40 ROUNDS FREE. (A $320.00 Added Value).

The Privilege: This brings your cost down to $6.06 per round. You are essentially shooting at our near‑wholesale cost, providing the club with the vital upfront capital needed to maintain facilities and inventory for 2026.
