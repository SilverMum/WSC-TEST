Trap & Skeet Ranges – General Information

Shooters wishing to shoot at WSC must complete the following points below (Non-members are welcomed as well):

1. Familiarize themselves with the WSC Range Safety Rules.
2. Review & sign the WSC Range Waiver Form on an annual basis.
3. Register and Sign-in at the range shack prior to shooting.
4. New members must attend a WSC Range Orientation and Safety Course at first opportunity after joining (a nominal one-time fee will apply).

Shooter Requirements

• All shooters must show proof of a Valid PAL.
• Non-PAL holders must be accompanied and supervised by a Valid PAL holder.
• Ear and eye protection is mandatory. (Limited number of loaners are available)

Ammunition Type

• Trap Range – #7½ or #8 size shot (Target Loads)
• Skeet Range – #9 size shot (Target Loads)

General Information

• Beginner and novice shooters are welcome. It is best to schedule a time for basic instruction and a review of safety policies & procedures.
• Reservations are available for private functions, stags, hunting clubs, meet and greets, etc.

Shooting Season

• Summer – May to end of October (approx.)
• Summer season may be extended if temperatures stay above freezing.
• Winter – Still in planning phase.

Club House

• Non-members welcome
• Restaurant & bar – Hours vary
• Please call or check the WSC website for current hours
• WSC Phone: 519.966.1600

Fees & Memberships

(Shooting fees are per round of 25 clay targets)

• WSC Members: $8.00 per round with a Trap & Skeet Associate Membership.
• Trap & Skeet Associate Membership: $100.00 annually (Available at Range Shack)
• Non-members: $10.00 per round + $10.00 day fee (Valid PAL required)
• WSC General Membership – available at the Range Shack
• Bulk shooting packages available (e.g., Buy 10 rounds, get 1 free)
• Youth/Student (18 or younger): $5.00 per round (No day fee)

Range Facilities: 1 Trap Field, 1 Skeet Field, 1 Pattern Board
• We do not sell shotgun shells
• We do not rent firearms

Range Rules

1. Safety glasses and hearing protection must be worn at all times.
2. All shooters MUST have a valid PAL or be supervised by a PAL holder.
3. No drugs or alcohol permitted.
4. Skeet Range: Max 12-gauge, min 26" barrel, #9 shot only.
5. Trap Range: Max 12-gauge, min 26" barrel, #7½ or #8 shot.
6. Maintain safe muzzle direction at all times.
7. After a misfire, keep action closed for 60 seconds.
8. Transport firearms PROVED safe, actions open.
9. Obey all RSO commands.
10. Carry firearms unloaded with action open.
11. Pick up hulls only after the round is complete.
12. Remove slings and accessories before shooting.

SAFETY AND COMMON SENSE ARE IMPORTANT!
When in doubt, ask questions and check it out!
