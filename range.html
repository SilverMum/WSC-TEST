Trap & Skeet – General Information

Windsor Sportsmen’s Club

Shooters wishing to shoot at WSC must complete the following (Non‑members welcome):

  1. Familiarize themselves with the WSC Range Safety Rules.
  2. Review and sign the WSC Range Waiver Form annually.
  3. Register and sign‑in at the range shack prior to shooting.
  4. New members must attend a WSC Range Orientation and Safety Course at the earliest opportunity (nominal one‑time fee applies).

Shooter Requirements

  • All shooters must show proof of a Valid PAL
  • Non‑PAL holders must be accompanied & supervised by a Valid PAL holder
  • Ear & eye protection is mandatory (Limited loaners available)


Ammunition Types

  • Trap Range: #7½ or #8 shot (Target Loads)
  • Skeet Range: #9 shot (Target Loads)


Range Rules

  1. Safety glasses & hearing protection required.
  2. All shooters must have a valid PAL or be supervised by a PAL holder.
  3. No drugs or alcohol in the vicinity of the ranges.
  4. Skeet Range:
    • Max 12‑gauge, min barrel 26"
    • Only 2¾" shells, #9 shot
    • Two shells allowed on Stations 1–7; one on Station 8
    • Actions open until in position
    • Tactical shotguns not permitted
  5. Trap Range:
    • Max 12‑gauge, min barrel 26"
    • Only 2¾" shells, #7½ shot or smaller
    • One shell loaded for singles
    • Tactical shotguns not permitted
  6. Maintain safe muzzle direction at all times.
  7. Misfire/hang‑fire: keep action closed 60 seconds, then inspect barrel.
  8. Transporting firearms: PROVED safe, action open, barrel up.
  9. Obey all RSO commands.
  10. Firearms unloaded with actions open when moving between stations.
  11. Pick up spent hulls only after firearm is in the rack.
  12. Remove slings & accessories before attending firing lines.
