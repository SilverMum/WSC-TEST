About the Range

Experience our exciting outdoor air rifle range! We offer a variety of paper and mechanical steel targets set at distances ranging from 10 to 20 meters. Perfect for junior shooters (10 years and up). Guardian must be present.

What to Expect

We provide a safe and fun environment for young shooters to learn and practice their skills.

The safety lessons learned here will ensure they enjoy shooting sports responsibly.

Participants take turns at the range.
One round (20 shots) per turn.

Experience the Difference: Our match-grade air rifles provide an excellent platform for skill development. Learn while having fun at Windsor Sportsmen's Air Rifle Range!

Range Details

10m Paper Targets

Shoot from bench-rest or unsupported positions. Analyze your results by comparing point of impact and group size.

10m–20m Knock-Down Targets

Steel targets with reset. Practice from sitting, kneeling or standing positions.

  • Develop core stability
  • Controlled breathing techniques
  • Target engagement skills
  • Challenge yourself to compensate for wind conditions!
