Windsor Sportsmen's Air Rifle Range

Sharpen Your Skills, Have Some Fun!
Windsor Sportsmen's Club, 2401 Dougall, Windsor, Ontario.
May 7th - September 10th.

About the Range

Experience our exciting outdoor air rifle range! We offer a variety of paper and mechanical steel targets set at distances ranging from 10 to 20 meters. Perfect for junior shooters (10 years and up), Guardian must be present.

Schedule

Wednesdays (weather permitting)
4:30 PM – 5:30 PM
5:30 PM – 6:30 PM

What to Expect

We provide a safe and fun environment for young shooters to learn and practice their skills. The safety lessons learned here will ensure they enjoy shooting sports responsibly.

Participants take turns at the range. One round (20 shots) per turn.

Pricing

Club Members: $3.00
Non‑Club Members: $5.00

Range Details

10m Paper Targets: Shoot from bench‑rest or unsupported positions. Analyze your results by comparing point of impact and group size.

10m‑20m Knock‑Down Targets: Steel targets with automatic reset. Practice from sitting, kneeling, prone, or standing positions. Develop core stability, controlled breathing, and target engagement skills. Challenge yourself to compensate for wind conditions!

Experience the Difference: Our match‑grade air rifles provide an excellent platform for skill development. Learn while having fun at Windsor Sportsmen's Air Rifle Range!
